DISCUSSION POINTS

Q4 Performance Review:
- Total Q4 revenue reached $558,000, exceeding target by 12%

- Services maintain steady growth trajectory

Regional Performance:

- Europe: $255,000 (46% of total), strong software sales

Product Analysis:

Challenges:

- Services attach rate needs improvement

2025 Planning:

- Explore partnership opportunities in Latin America
