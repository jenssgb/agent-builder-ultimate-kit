WIN/LOSS ANALYSIS
=============================================================================

OVERALL WIN RATES (Last 12 Months):
- Total Opportunities: 850
- Wins: 380 (45%)
- Losses: 420 (49%)
- No Decision: 50 (6%)

WIN RATE BY COMPETITOR:
vs. Competitor A: 52% (we win more than we lose)
vs. Competitor B: 38% (they win more than we lose)
vs. Competitor C: 68% (we dominate head-to-head)
vs. Others: 44%

WHY WE WIN:

Top Reasons (from win/loss interviews):
1. Ease of use (cited by 78% of wins)
   - "Team actually wanted to use it"
   - "No training required"
   - "Adopted across company in weeks"

2. Cost/Value (cited by 62% of wins)
   - "Better ROI than alternatives"
   - "Transparent, predictable pricing"
   - "Got more for less"

3. Support Quality (cited by 55% of wins)
   - "15-minute response time vs. hours/days with others"
   - "Felt like a partner, not just vendor"
   - "Got us live in record time"

4. Modern Platform (cited by 48% of wins)
   - "Clean, modern interface"
   - "Frequent updates and new features"
   - "Felt like the future"

5. Enterprise + Ease (cited by 41% of wins)
   - "Best of both worlds"
   - "Security we need, usability we want"
   - "No compromise required"

WHY WE LOSE:

Top Reasons (from win/loss interviews):
1. Missing Features (cited by 38% of losses)
   - "Lacked specific feature we needed"
   - "Not as comprehensive as [Competitor A]"
   - "Integration we required wasn't available"

2. Price (cited by 32% of losses)
   - "Competitor offered discount we couldn't match"
   - "Cheaper option was 'good enough'"
   - "Budget cut, went with lower-cost option"

3. Existing Relationship (cited by 28% of losses)
   - "Already using [Competitor] for other products"
   - "Multi-year contract in place"
   - "Incumbent advantage"

4. Risk Aversion (cited by 22% of losses)
   - "Went with market leader to be safe"
   - "Chose more established vendor"
   - "Not ready to take a chance on newer player"

5. PE/Political (cited by 18% of losses)
   - "Executive mandate to use [Competitor]"
   - "Procurement preferred list only"
   - "Internal champion pushed different solution"

ACTIONS BASED ON WIN/LOSS:
‚úì Product: Prioritize most-requested missing features
‚úì Pricing: Defend value, create "match competitor" fund for strategic deals
‚úì Marketing: More enterprise logos, customer stories, analyst relations
