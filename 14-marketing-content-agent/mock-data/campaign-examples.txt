CAMPAIGN FRAMEWORKS & TEMPLATES
=============================================================================

CAMPAIGN BRIEF TEMPLATE:

1. Campaign Overview
   - Name, timeline, owner
   - Primary goal (SMART)
   - Budget and resources

2. Target Audience
   - Primary persona(s)
   - Company size, industry, role
   - Pain points and goals

3. Key Messages
   - Primary value proposition
   - 3-5 supporting messages
   - Proof points

4. Creative Strategy
   - Core creative concept
   - Key visuals and messaging
   - Tone and style

5. Tactics & Channels
   - Owned, earned, paid breakdown
   - Channel-specific tactics
   - Timeline/Gantt chart

6. Budget Allocation
   - By channel
   - By tactic
   - Contingency (10-15%)

7. Success Metrics
   - Primary KPIs
   - Secondary metrics
   - Measurement plan

8. Assets Needed
   - Creative assets (list)
   - Content assets (list)
   - Technical requirements

9. Team & Responsibilities
   - Project owner
   - Contributors
   - Approvers

10. Risks & Mitigation
    - Potential issues
    - Backup plans

CAMPAIGN NAMING CONVENTION:
[Year]-[Quarter]-[Type]-[Brief Description]
Example: 2024-Q2-LAUNCH-New-Enterprise-Features
