=============================================================================
CONTENT CREATION PROCESS
=============================================================================

WORKFLOW:

1. IDEATION (Week 1)
- Review content calendar gaps
- Analyze search trends and keywords
- Monitor competitor content
- Gather customer feedback themes
- Brainstorm topics in team meeting

2. PLANNING (Week 1-2)
- Select topics based on priority
- Assign writers and subject matter experts
- Define content format and length
- Identify keywords and SEO requirements
- Schedule publication dates

3. CREATION (Week 2-3)
- Research topic thoroughly
- Outline content structure
- Write first draft
- Create supporting visuals (charts, screenshots)
- Add internal/external links

4. REVIEW (Week 3)
- Editor reviews for clarity, grammar, brand voice
- SME reviews for technical accuracy
- SEO review (keywords, metadata, structure)
- Legal review (if claims or customer data)
- Final approval from Content Director

5. OPTIMIZATION (Week 3-4)
- Add meta title and description
- Optimize images (compress, add alt text)
- Format for readability (headings, bullets, short paragraphs)
- Add internal links to related content
- Set up tracking (UTM codes, analytics)

6. PUBLICATION (Week 4)
- Publish on scheduled date
- Distribute across channels (email, social)
- Update content hub and navigation
- Monitor for errors or issues

7. PROMOTION (Week 4-6)
- Share on social media (multiple times)
- Include in email newsletter
- Paid promotion (if budget allocated)
- Outreach to influencers/partners
- Repurpose into other formats

8. ANALYSIS (Ongoing)
- Track performance metrics
- Identify high/low performers
- Update and refresh underperforming content
- Double down on what works
- Monthly content performance review

CONTENT GUIDELINES
=============================================================================

TONE & VOICE:
✓ Conversational but professional
✓ Confident but not arrogant
✓ Helpful and empathetic
✓ Clear and concise
✓ Data-driven with stories

WRITING BEST PRACTICES:

Structure:
- Hook in first 2 sentences
- Clear subheadings (scannable)
- Short paragraphs (3-4 sentences)
- Bullet points for lists
- Conclusion with clear CTA

Length:
- Blog posts: 1,500-2,500 words (long-form)
- How-tos: 1,000-1,500 words
- Listicles: 800-1,200 words
- Case studies: 800-1,000 words
- Social posts: Platform-specific guidelines

SEO:
✓ Primary keyword in title, first paragraph, conclusion
✓ Secondary keywords naturally throughout
✓ Internal links to 3-5 related pages
✓ External links to authoritative sources
✓ Optimize images with alt text
✓ Use schema markup where applicable

Credibility:
✓ Cite sources and link to studies
✓ Include data and statistics
✓ Add customer quotes and examples
✓ Show credentials of authors
✓ Update publish/modified dates
