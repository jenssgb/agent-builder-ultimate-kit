**Version**: 3.0  
**Effective Date**: January 1, 2024  
**Owner**: Finance Department  
**Approved By**: Chief Financial Officer

---

## Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) establishes the process for submitting, approving, and processing employee expense reimbursements at Contoso Corporation.

---

## Scope

This SOP applies to all Contoso employees who incur business-related expenses that require reimbursement.

---

## Policy Summary

Contoso will reimburse employees for reasonable and necessary business expenses incurred while performing their job duties. All expenses must be:
- Business-related and necessary
- Properly documented with receipts
- Submitted within 30 days of expense date
- Pre-approved if required (travel, large purchases)
- Compliant with IRS regulations

---

## Reimbursable Expenses

### Category 1: Travel Expenses
**Airfare**:
- Coach class for flights under 6 hours
- Premium economy or business class requires VP approval for flights over 6 hours
- Book at least 14 days in advance when possible
- Use preferred travel vendors (American Airlines, United, Delta)

**Lodging**:
- Standard room at business-class hotels
- Maximum rates: $250/night (major cities), $150/night (other locations)
- Exceeding limits requires manager approval
- Use preferred hotel chains (Marriott, Hilton, Hyatt)

**Ground Transportation**:
- Taxi, Uber, Lyft for airport transfers and client meetings
- Rental cars approved for multi-day trips or locations without public transit
- Mileage reimbursement: $0.67 per mile (2024 IRS rate) for personal vehicle use
- Parking fees at airports, hotels, client sites

**Meals During Travel**:
- Breakfast: Up to $15
- Lunch: Up to $25
- Dinner: Up to $50
- Daily maximum: $90
- Alcohol: Not reimbursed (unless client entertainment pre-approved)

### Category 2: Client Entertainment
**Meals with Clients**:
- Pre-approval required for meals over $100 per person
- Must include client names and company on expense report
- Alcohol: Up to 2 drinks per person, manager approval required

**Entertainment Events**:
- Sporting events, concerts, theater: CFO approval required
- Must have clear business purpose
- Maximum: $500 per event

### Category 3: Office Supplies and Equipment
**Office Supplies**:
- Pens, notebooks, folders, etc.: Up to $50/month without approval
- Over $50: Manager approval required

**Computer Equipment**:
- Keyboard, mouse, monitor: IT department approval required
- Purchases over $200: Must use IT procurement process

**Software and Subscriptions**:
- Business software: Manager approval required
- Subscriptions over $100/year: Manager and director approval required

### Category 4: Professional Development
**Training and Conferences**:
- Conference registration: Up to $2,000/year with manager approval
- Travel and lodging: Follow standard travel guidelines
- Pre-approval required for all conferences

**Certifications**:
- Exam fees: Reimbursed upon successful completion
- Study materials: Up to $500 with manager approval
- Must be relevant to current role

**Memberships**:
- Professional organization memberships: Up to $300/year
- Must be job-related and approved by manager

### Category 5: Communication
**Mobile Phone**:
- Monthly reimbursement: $30 for use of personal phone for business
- No receipts required (fixed monthly amount)

**Internet (Remote Workers)**:
- Monthly reimbursement: $50 with proof of service
- Submit internet bill monthly

### Category 6: Remote Work Equipment
**One-Time Home Office Setup**:
- Office chair, desk, lighting: Up to $500 one-time allowance
- Must submit receipts within 90 days of remote work approval
- Approved through HR, reimbursed by Finance

---

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following expenses will **NOT** be reimbursed:
❌ Personal expenses (magazines, toiletries, gym memberships)  
❌ Alcoholic beverages (except approved client entertainment)  
❌ Fines and penalties (parking tickets, speeding tickets)  
❌ Personal entertainment (in-room movies, mini-bar)  
❌ Upgrades not pre-approved (car rental, hotel room, flight)  
❌ Expenses for family members or companions  
❌ Commuting to regular work location  
❌ Expenses over 90 days old

## Expense Submission Process

### Step 1: Gather Documentation
**Required for All Expenses**:
- Original itemized receipt (not just credit card slip)
- Date of expense
- Vendor/merchant name
- Amount paid
- Description of business purpose

**Additional Requirements**:
- Meals: List attendees and their companies
- Mileage: Start/end locations and odometer readings
- Client entertainment: Names, companies, business purpose
- Conference: Agenda or program showing dates

### Step 2: Access Expense System
**Login**: https://expenses.contoso.com
- Use your Contoso credentials
- Works on desktop and mobile devices
- Mobile app: "Contoso Expenses" (iOS and Android)

### Step 3: Create Expense Report
**Required Fields**:
- Report name (e.g., "November 2024 Travel")
- Cost center/department code
- Project code (if applicable)
- Report period dates

**Add Expense Lines**:
- Click "Add Expense"
- Select expense category (Travel, Meals, Office Supplies, etc.)
- Enter date, merchant, amount, business purpose
- Attach receipt photo or PDF

**Itemization**:
- Hotel folios: Must show itemized charges (not just total)
- Group meals: List each attendee separately
- Multi-day trips: Create separate expense line for each day

### Step 4: Attach Receipts
**Accepted Formats**:
- Photo/scan of paper receipt (JPG, PNG, PDF)
- Electronic receipt (email confirmation, PDF invoice)
- Credit card statement (only if original receipt lost)

**Photo Requirements**:
- All information clearly visible
- No glare or shadows
- Straighten and crop image
- File size under 5MB

**Lost Receipt Affidavit**:
- For receipts under $25: Written explanation accepted
- For receipts over $25: Lost Receipt Form required (manager signature)
- Frequent lost receipts may result in non-reimbursement

### Step 5: Submit for Approval
- Review all entries for accuracy
- Check that all receipts are attached
- Click "Submit" to send to manager
- Receive email confirmation with report number

## Approval Workflow

### Manager Approval (Level 1)
**Timeline**: Manager has 5 business days to review and approve

**Manager Reviews**:
- Expense is business-related and necessary
- Amount is reasonable and within policy limits
- Receipts are attached and legible
- Business purpose is clearly stated

**Manager Actions**:
- **Approve**: Expense moves to Finance for processing
- **Reject**: Returns to employee with explanation
- **Request More Info**: Employee provides clarification

### Director Approval (Level 2)
**Required For**:
- Expenses over $5,000
- Conference registrations over $2,000
- Client entertainment over $500
- Any out-of-policy expenses

**Timeline**: Director has 3 business days to review

### Finance Review (Level 3)
**Finance Team Reviews**:
- Compliance with IRS regulations
- Proper documentation attached
- Coding is correct (cost center, project)
- No duplicate submissions

**Finance Actions**:
- **Approve for Payment**: Moves to payment processing
- **Return to Manager**: For additional approval or clarification
- **Reject**: Returns to employee with explanation

---

## Payment Processing

### Processing Timeline
- **Standard processing**: 7-10 business days after final approval
- **Direct deposit**: Funds deposited to bank account on file
- **First-time reimbursement**: 10-14 business days (bank verification)

### Payment Schedule
- Expenses approved Monday-Wednesday: Paid Friday of same week
- Expenses approved Thursday-Friday: Paid Friday of following week

### Payment Notification
- Email notification when payment is processed
- Check pay stub for "Expense Reimbursement" line item
- Contact Finance if payment not received within 15 business days

## Special Procedures

### Corporate Credit Card Expenses
If you have a corporate credit card (Amex or Visa):
- Submit expenses through same expense system
- Select "Corporate Card" as payment method
- Reconcile card statement monthly
- Expense report due 10 days after statement close
- Personal charges not permitted on corporate card

### International Travel
**Additional Requirements**:
- Pre-approval required from director
- Currency conversion: Use rate from expense date
- VAT/GST receipts: Keep separate for tax reclaim
- Per diem rates: Follow U.S. State Department rates by country

**International Meal Rates**: https://aoprals.state.gov/web920/per_diem.asp

### Group Travel/Team Events
**Manager Paying for Team**:
- One expense report for entire group
- List all attendees and their departments
- Obtain pre-approval from director for costs over $500
- Split costs across department budgets if needed

### Relocation Expenses
**New Hire Relocation**:
- Separate process managed by HR
- Contact hr@contoso.com for relocation policy
- Not submitted through standard expense system

## Policy Violations and Exceptions

### Common Violations
- Submitting personal expenses as business expenses
- Exceeding per diem limits without approval
- Missing or inadequate receipts
- Late submissions (over 30 days)
- Duplicate submissions

### Consequences
**First Violation**: Written warning, expense returned for correction  
**Second Violation**: Manager counseling, possible delayed reimbursement  
**Third Violation**: Disciplinary action, loss of expense privileges  
**Intentional Fraud**: Termination of employment, possible legal action

### Exception Requests
**Out-of-Policy Expenses**:
- Submit written justification with expense report
- Requires director or VP approval
- Must be truly exceptional circumstances
- Not guaranteed to be approved

**Examples of Valid Exceptions**:
- Emergency travel (family illness, business crisis)
- Last-minute conference attendance (no advance booking)
- Travel to high-cost location (NYC, San Francisco, London)

## FAQs

**Q: How long do I have to submit an expense report?**  
A: 30 days from the date of expense. Expenses over 30 days old may not be reimbursed.

**Q: What if I lost a receipt?**  
A: For expenses under $25, provide written explanation. For expenses over $25, complete Lost Receipt Affidavit with manager signature.

**Q: Can I submit expenses from a previous job?**  
A: No. Only expenses incurred while employed at Contoso are reimbursable.

**Q: What if my manager is on vacation?**  
A: Expense reports automatically route to manager's backup approver after 5 days.

**Q: Can I get an advance for travel expenses?**  
A: Travel advances available for international trips or trips over $2,000. Request through Finance at least 2 weeks before travel.

**Q: What if I accidentally used my personal credit card for a corporate expense?**  
A: Submit for reimbursement through normal process. Select "Personal Card" as payment method.

**Q: How do I update my direct deposit information?**  
A: Update bank account information in HR portal under "Payroll Settings". Changes take effect next pay cycle.

**Q: What if my expense report was rejected?**  
A: Review rejection reason in email notification. Make corrections and resubmit. Contact your manager if you have questions.

**Q: Can I expense meals if I forgot to get a receipt?**  
A: Not typically. However, if under $25 and infrequent, manager may approve with written explanation.

**Q: Are tips included in meal per diem limits?**  
A: Yes. The per diem limits include tax and tip (15-20% suggested).
