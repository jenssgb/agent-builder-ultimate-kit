## 1. Purpose and Scope

This policy establishes guidelines for remote work arrangements at Contoso Corporation. It applies to all full-time and part-time employees who have completed their probationary period and whose roles are suitable for remote work.

**Policy Objective**: To provide flexible work arrangements that support work-life balance, increase productivity, and reduce environmental impact while maintaining business operations and team collaboration.

---

## 2. Eligibility Criteria

### 2.1 Employee Requirements
Employees must meet ALL of the following criteria to be eligible for remote work:

✅ **Tenure**: Completed 90-day probationary period  
✅ **Performance**: Met or exceeded performance expectations in last review  
✅ **Role Suitability**: Job responsibilities can be performed remotely  
✅ **Manager Approval**: Direct supervisor has approved remote work arrangement  
✅ **Technology**: Has reliable internet and appropriate home workspace

### 2.2 Role Suitability Assessment
**Suitable Roles** (examples):
- Software developers and engineers
- Data analysts and scientists
- Content writers and designers
- Customer support representatives
- Project managers
- Sales representatives

**Unsuitable Roles** (examples):
- Facilities and maintenance staff
- Reception and mail room staff
- Roles requiring specialized on-site equipment
- Positions with regular in-person customer interactions
- Lab technicians and researchers

### 2.3 Ineligibility Factors
Remote work may be denied or revoked if:
- Performance issues or concerns exist
- Role requires frequent in-person collaboration
- Security or confidentiality concerns
- Business continuity requirements
- Inadequate home workspace or technology

## 3. Remote Work Arrangements

### 3.1 Hybrid Remote Work
**Definition**: Combination of in-office and remote work days

**Standard Hybrid Schedule**:
- **2 days in office** + **3 days remote** per week (most common)
- **3 days in office** + **2 days remote** per week (collaboration-focused roles)
- **1 day in office** + **4 days remote** per week (independent contributor roles)

**In-Office Days**:
- Typical in-office days: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday (team collaboration days)
- Flexible days: Monday and Friday (common remote days)
- Must attend mandatory team meetings and events

### 3.2 Full Remote Work
**Definition**: Work from home 100% of the time

**Requirements**:
- Role has minimal need for in-person collaboration
- Employee has demonstrated strong remote work performance
- Manager and skip-level manager approval required
- Must travel to office for quarterly meetings and annual events
- May be location-dependent (must reside within country of employment)

**Travel Expectations**:
- Quarterly in-person team meetings (1-2 days)
- Annual company gatherings (3-5 days)
- Project-specific meetings as needed
- Travel expenses covered by company

### 3.3 Flexible Remote Work
**Definition**: Variable schedule based on business needs

**Usage**:
- Ad-hoc remote days with manager approval
- Available to all eligible employees
- Minimum 24-hour notice preferred
- Ideal for appointments, home deliveries, or focused work time

## 4. Technology and Equipment

### 4.1 Company-Provided Equipment
All remote employees receive:

✅ **Laptop** (Windows or Mac based on role)  
✅ **Docking station** (for dual monitor setup)  
✅ **Monitor** (24" or 27", one monitor standard, second upon request)  
✅ **Keyboard and mouse** (wireless)  
✅ **Headset with microphone** (noise-canceling)  
✅ **Webcam** (HD quality, if laptop camera insufficient)  
✅ **Phone** (mobile device or VoIP softphone)

### 4.2 Home Office Stipend
**One-Time Setup Allowance**: $500

Can be used for:
- Ergonomic office chair
- Desk or standing desk converter
- Monitor arm or laptop stand
- Lighting or acoustic improvements
- Printer (if role requires)

**Process**: Submit receipts to HR for reimbursement within first 90 days of remote work approval.

### 4.3 Recurring Allowances
**Internet Reimbursement**: $50 per month (requires proof of service)  
**Phone Reimbursement**: $30 per month (if using personal phone for work)  
**Utilities**: Not reimbursed

**Submission**: Use expense report system monthly with supporting documentation.

### 4.4 Technical Requirements

**Minimum Internet Speed**:
- Download: 25 Mbps minimum, 50 Mbps recommended
- Upload: 10 Mbps minimum, 25 Mbps recommended
- Ping/Latency: Under 50ms preferred

**Network Security**:
- Secure WiFi with WPA2 or WPA3 encryption
- Separate network from guest devices (recommended)
- VPN required for accessing internal systems
- Do not work from public WiFi networks

## 5. Workspace Requirements

### 5.1 Home Office Standards
Remote employees must maintain a dedicated workspace that is:

✅ **Quiet**: Free from excessive noise and distractions  
✅ **Private**: Confidential conversations can be conducted  
✅ **Well-lit**: Adequate lighting for video calls  
✅ **Ergonomic**: Proper desk and chair setup  
✅ **Safe**: Free from hazards, meets basic safety standards

### 5.2 Video Call Background
- Professional background (plain wall, bookshelf, or virtual background)
- No inappropriate or distracting items visible
- Good lighting (face clearly visible)
- Professional appearance required for client-facing calls

### 5.3 Workspace Inspection
- Company reserves right to request photos of home workspace
- May be required for safety or ergonomic assessment
- Facilities team can provide ergonomic consultation

## 6. Work Hours and Availability

### 6.1 Core Hours
**Required Availability**: 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM local time (Monday-Friday)

During core hours, employees must:
- Be available for meetings and calls
- Respond to messages within 15 minutes
- Have video capability for scheduled meetings
- Be actively working (not running errands or personal tasks)

### 6.2 Flexible Hours
- Start time: Between 7:00 AM - 10:00 AM
- End time: Between 4:00 PM - 7:00 PM
- Total: 8 hours work + 30-60 min lunch break
- Document schedule in Outlook calendar

### 6.3 Status and Availability
**Teams Status Management**:
- Set status to "Available" during work hours
- Set status to "In a meeting" when busy
- Set status to "Do not disturb" for focused work (max 2 hours)
- Set status to "Away" for lunch or breaks
- Set status to "Off work" outside business hours

**Calendar Management**:
- Keep Outlook calendar updated
- Block focus time for deep work
- Accept/decline meeting invitations promptly
- Mark working location: "Office" or "Remote"

## 7. Communication and Collaboration

### 7.1 Communication Expectations
- **Email**: Respond within 4 business hours
- **Teams Chat**: Respond within 15 minutes during core hours
- **Phone/Video Calls**: Answer promptly during core hours
- **Voicemail**: Return calls within 4 business hours

### 7.2 Meeting Etiquette
**Video Calls**:
- Camera ON for team meetings and one-on-ones
- Camera optional for large department meetings (50+ people)
- Professional appearance (business casual minimum)
- Mute when not speaking
- Test audio/video before important meetings

**Meeting Participation**:
- Join meetings on time
- Minimize multitasking
- Contribute actively to discussions
- Take notes or use meeting transcription
- Follow up on action items

### 7.3 Team Collaboration
- Use Teams channels for team discussions
- Use SharePoint for document collaboration
- Attend team-building activities (virtual or in-person)
- Maintain regular check-ins with manager (weekly minimum)

## 8. Performance and Accountability

### 8.1 Performance Expectations
Remote employees are held to the same performance standards as office-based employees:

- Meet all deadlines and commitments
- Maintain quality of work
- Achieve performance goals and objectives
- Participate in team activities
- Complete required training

### 8.2 Productivity Monitoring
- Performance measured by results and outcomes, not hours logged
- Regular check-ins with manager (weekly or bi-weekly)
- Use of project management tools for transparency
- Time tracking required for certain roles (e.g., billable hours)

### 8.3 Performance Issues
If performance concerns arise:
1. Manager schedules discussion meeting
2. Performance improvement plan may be implemented
3. Remote work privileges may be temporarily suspended
4. Return to full-time office work may be required

---

## 9. Security and Confidentiality

### 9.1 Data Protection
- Use company-provided VPN for all work activities
- Do not store confidential data on personal devices
- Lock computer when stepping away (Windows + L or Cmd + Ctrl + Q)
- Use screen privacy filter for work in public spaces
- Shred confidential documents or return to office for secure disposal

### 9.2 Physical Security
- Keep company equipment secure and locked when not in use
- Do not allow family members or visitors to use company devices
- Report lost or stolen equipment immediately (within 2 hours)
- Do not leave equipment visible in vehicles

### 9.3 Confidential Conversations
- Ensure privacy for confidential calls and meetings
- Use headphones for sensitive conversations
- Close doors or move to private space as needed
- Do not discuss confidential matters in public spaces

---

## 10. Health and Safety

### 10.1 Ergonomics
- Set up workstation according to ergonomic guidelines (see HR portal)
- Take regular breaks (5 minutes every hour, 30 minutes every 4 hours)
- Use proper posture and positioning
- Request ergonomic assessment if experiencing discomfort (contact HR)

### 10.2 Injury Reporting
- Report work-related injuries immediately to manager and HR
- Complete incident report within 24 hours
- Workers' compensation covers home office injuries during work hours
- Document circumstances and take photos if applicable

### 10.3 Mental Health and Wellbeing
- Maintain work-life boundaries (end work at designated time)
- Take regular breaks and use vacation time
- Stay connected with team members
- Access Employee Assistance Program (EAP) if needed: 1-800-555-0123

## 11. Approval Process

### 11.1 How to Request Remote Work

**Step 1**: Discuss with your manager
- Explain desired arrangement (hybrid, full remote, flexible)
- Confirm role suitability and business needs

**Step 2**: Complete Remote Work Request Form
- Access form on HR portal
- Provide details of requested arrangement
- Describe home workspace setup

**Step 3**: Manager Review and Approval
- Manager evaluates request against eligibility criteria
- May request additional information or workspace photos
- Approval decision within 5 business days

**Step 4**: HR Processing
- HR reviews and processes approved requests
- IT provisions equipment and access
- Schedule equipment pickup or delivery

**Step 5**: Begin Remote Work
- Attend remote work orientation session
- Sign remote work agreement
- Receive equipment and setup support

### 11.2 Trial Period
- Initial 90-day trial period for all new remote work arrangements
- Manager and employee evaluate effectiveness
- Adjustments made as needed
- Arrangement may be modified or terminated if not working

## 13. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I work from a different location (e.g., vacation rental, family member's home)?**  
A: Occasional remote work from alternate locations within your country of employment is permitted with manager approval. Extended work from alternate locations (>2 weeks) requires HR approval. International remote work requires legal and tax review.

**Q: What if my internet goes down?**  
A: Notify your manager immediately. Use mobile hotspot if available, or go to the office if feasible. Ongoing connectivity issues may affect remote work eligibility.

**Q: Can I work from a coffee shop or coworking space?**  
A: Use of public spaces should be limited and approved by manager. Use VPN and screen privacy filter. Do not conduct confidential calls or handle sensitive data in public.

**Q: What if I need to bring my child to meetings due to school closure?**  
A: Communicate with your manager. Use flexibility and understanding during emergencies. Ongoing childcare during work hours is employee's responsibility.

**Q: How do I expense my internet bill?**  
A: Submit monthly expense report with copy of internet bill showing provider and amount. Reimbursement is $50/month regardless of actual bill amount.
