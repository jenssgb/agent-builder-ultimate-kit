## 2. Employment Basics

### 2.1 Employment Classification
- **Full-time employees**: 40 hours per week
- **Part-time employees**: Less than 30 hours per week
- **Contract employees**: Fixed-term assignments

### 2.2 Probationary Period
New employees serve a 90-day probationary period during which performance is closely evaluated.

### 2.3 Equal Employment Opportunity
Contoso is an equal opportunity employer. We do not discriminate based on race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, or any other protected characteristic.

## 3. Work Schedule and Remote Work

### 3.1 Standard Work Hours
- Core business hours: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM local time
- Flexible start times available: 7:00 AM - 10:00 AM
- Lunch break: 30-60 minutes (unpaid)

### 3.2 Remote Work Policy (Updated 2024)
**Eligibility**:
- Employees who have completed probationary period
- Role must be suitable for remote work
- Manager approval required

**Remote Work Options**:
- Hybrid: 2-3 days in office, 2-3 days remote
- Full remote: Available for approved roles
- Flexible: Varies by team and business needs

**Equipment Provided**:
- Laptop and docking station
- Monitor (upon request)
- Headset and webcam
- Office chair allowance ($500)

**Requirements**:
- Reliable high-speed internet connection
- Dedicated workspace
- Availability during core business hours
- Attendance at in-person meetings as required

## 4. Compensation and Benefits

### 4.1 Payroll
- Pay frequency: Bi-weekly (every other Friday)
- Direct deposit required
- Access pay stubs via HR portal

### 4.2 Performance Reviews
- Annual performance reviews in December
- Mid-year check-ins in June
- Merit increases based on performance and budget

### 4.3 Health Insurance
**Medical Insurance**:
- Coverage begins first day of the month after hire date
- Three plan options: Basic, Standard, Premium
- Company pays 80% of Basic plan premium
- Employee pays difference for Standard/Premium plans

**Dental Insurance**:
- Included with medical coverage
- Preventive care covered 100%

**Vision Insurance**:
- Optional add-on ($15/month)
- Annual eye exam and frames/lenses covered

### 4.4 Retirement Plan (401k)
- Eligibility: After 90 days of employment
- Employee contribution: Up to IRS limit
- Company match: 50% of first 6% contributed
- Vesting schedule: 4-year graded vesting

### 4.5 Other Benefits
- Life insurance: 1x annual salary (company-paid)
- Disability insurance: Short-term and long-term
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP): Confidential counseling
- Commuter benefits: Pre-tax parking and transit
- Gym membership discount: 25% off selected facilities

## 5. Time Off and Leave Policies

### 5.1 Vacation Time (PTO)
**Accrual Rates**:
- Years 0-2: 15 days per year (1.25 days/month)
- Years 3-5: 20 days per year (1.67 days/month)
- Years 6+: 25 days per year (2.08 days/month)

**Usage**:
- Must be requested and approved in advance
- Minimum increment: 4 hours
- Carryover limit: 5 days maximum to next year

### 5.2 Sick Leave
- 10 days per year (accrued monthly)
- Can be used for illness, medical appointments, or family care
- Unused sick leave carries over annually (no cap)

### 5.3 Holidays
Contoso observes 11 paid holidays:
- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day
- Presidents' Day
- Memorial Day
- Juneteenth
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving Day
- Day after Thanksgiving
- Christmas Eve
- Christmas Day

### 5.4 Parental Leave
- **Maternity leave**: 16 weeks paid
- **Paternity leave**: 8 weeks paid
- **Adoption leave**: 12 weeks paid
- Must be taken within first year of birth/adoption

### 5.5 Bereavement Leave
- 5 days paid for immediate family member
- 3 days paid for extended family member

### 5.6 Jury Duty
- Paid leave for duration of jury service
- Must provide jury summons to HR

## 6. Code of Conduct

### 6.1 Professional Standards
All employees must:
- Treat colleagues with respect and dignity
- Maintain confidentiality of company information
- Avoid conflicts of interest
- Report unethical behavior
- Comply with all laws and regulations

### 6.2 Conflicts of Interest
Employees must disclose:
- Outside employment or business interests
- Financial interests in competitors or vendors
- Personal relationships with subordinates or vendors

**Disclosure Process**: Submit Conflict of Interest Form to HR within 30 days of situation arising.

### 6.3 Confidentiality
Employees have access to confidential information including:
- Customer data and contracts
- Financial information
- Product development plans
- Personnel information

**Requirements**:
- Sign confidentiality agreement upon hire
- Do not discuss confidential information outside work
- Protect documents and data from unauthorized access
- Return all confidential materials upon termination

### 6.4 Anti-Harassment Policy
Contoso prohibits harassment of any kind, including:
- Sexual harassment
- Bullying or intimidation
- Discrimination based on protected characteristics

**Reporting**: Contact HR, your manager, or use anonymous hotline: 1-800-555-0199

## 7. Workplace Safety

### 7.1 Health and Safety
- Report all injuries immediately to supervisor
- Follow safety protocols for your role
- Participate in emergency drills
- Use personal protective equipment when required

### 7.2 Ergonomics
- Request ergonomic assessment if experiencing discomfort
- Adjust workstation to proper height and distance
- Take regular breaks from computer work

### 7.3 Emergency Procedures
- Know location of fire exits and assembly points
- Report safety hazards to Facilities
- Keep emergency contact information current in HR system

## 8. Technology and Equipment

### 8.1 Company Equipment
- Laptop, phone, and accessories are company property
- Use equipment for business purposes only
- Report lost or stolen equipment immediately
- Return all equipment upon termination

### 8.2 Acceptable Use Policy
**Permitted**:
- Business communications
- Reasonable personal use during breaks
- Professional development and learning

**Prohibited**:
- Downloading unauthorized software
- Accessing inappropriate websites
- Sharing login credentials
- Using company resources for personal business

### 8.3 Data Security
- Use strong passwords (12+ characters, changed every 90 days)
- Enable multi-factor authentication
- Lock computer when away from desk
- Do not store sensitive data on personal devices
- Report suspected security incidents immediately

### 8.4 Social Media
**Personal Social Media**:
- Do not speak on behalf of Contoso without authorization
- Do not share confidential company information
- Include disclaimer if discussing work-related topics
- Be respectful and professional

**Company Social Media**:
- Only authorized employees may post on company accounts
- Follow brand guidelines and approval process

## 9. Separation of Employment

### 9.1 Resignation
- Provide 2 weeks' notice in writing
- Return all company property
- Complete exit interview
- Final paycheck includes unused vacation time

### 9.2 Termination
- Company reserves right to terminate at-will employment
- Severance pay may be offered based on circumstances
- COBRA benefits information provided
