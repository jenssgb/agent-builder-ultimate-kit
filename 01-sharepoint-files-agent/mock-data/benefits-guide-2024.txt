## Benefits Overview

Contoso offers a comprehensive benefits package to support the health, financial security, and well-being of our employees and their families.

### Benefits Summary
- Medical, Dental, and Vision Insurance
- 401(k) Retirement Plan with Company Match
- Paid Time Off (PTO) and Holidays
- Life and Disability Insurance
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
- Professional Development
- Wellness Programs

## Section 1: Medical Insurance

### Plan Options
Contoso offers three medical insurance plan options through BlueCross BlueShield:

**Plan A - Basic PPO**
- Monthly Premium: Employee pays 20%, Company pays 80%
- Annual Deductible: $1,500 individual / $3,000 family
- Out-of-Pocket Maximum: $5,000 individual / $10,000 family
- Office Visit Copay: $30
- Specialist Copay: $50
- Emergency Room: $250 copay
- Prescription Drugs: $10/$30/$60 (generic/preferred/non-preferred)

**Plan B - Standard PPO**
- Monthly Premium: Employee pays 30%, Company pays 70%
- Annual Deductible: $1,000 individual / $2,000 family
- Out-of-Pocket Maximum: $3,500 individual / $7,000 family
- Office Visit Copay: $20
- Specialist Copay: $40
- Emergency Room: $150 copay
- Prescription Drugs: $10/$25/$50

**Plan C - Premium PPO**
- Monthly Premium: Employee pays 40%, Company pays 60%
- Annual Deductible: $500 individual / $1,000 family
- Out-of-Pocket Maximum: $2,000 individual / $4,000 family
- Office Visit Copay: $10
- Specialist Copay: $25
- Emergency Room: $100 copay
- Prescription Drugs: $5/$15/$30

### Coverage Details
- Preventive care: 100% covered (annual physical, immunizations, screenings)
- Hospitalization: Covered after deductible
- Surgery: Covered after deductible
- Mental health services: Same as medical coverage
- Maternity care: Covered after deductible
- Prescription mail order: 90-day supply at 2x copay

### Enrollment
- Coverage begins: First day of month following hire date
- New hires: 30 days to enroll from start date
- Annual enrollment: November for following year
- Qualifying life events: 30 days to make changes

## Section 2: Dental Insurance

### Dental Plan (Included with Medical)
- Provider: Delta Dental PPO
- Monthly Premium: Included in medical premium
- Annual Maximum Benefit: $2,000 per person

**Coverage Levels**:
- Preventive (cleanings, exams, X-rays): 100% covered, no deductible
- Basic (fillings, extractions): 80% covered after $50 deductible
- Major (crowns, root canals, bridges): 50% covered after $50 deductible
- Orthodontia: 50% covered, $1,500 lifetime maximum

**Visit Frequency**:
- Routine cleanings: 2 per year
- Exams: 2 per year
- X-rays: 1 set per year

## Section 3: Vision Insurance

### Vision Plan (Optional Add-On)
- Provider: VSP Vision Care
- Monthly Premium: $15 per employee / $30 per family
- Enrollment: Optional during open enrollment

**Coverage**:
- Eye exam: Covered once per year ($10 copay)
- Lenses: Covered once per year ($25 copay)
  - Single, bifocal, or progressive lenses
  - Polycarbonate lenses for children
- Frames: $150 allowance every 2 years ($130 allowance)
- Contact lenses: $150 allowance (in lieu of glasses)
- Lens enhancements: 20-30% discount

**Out-of-Network Benefits**:
- Eye exam: Up to $45 reimbursement
- Frames: Up to $70 reimbursement
- Lenses: Up to $30-$50 reimbursement

## Section 4: 401(k) Retirement Plan

### Plan Details
- Provider: Fidelity Investments
- Eligibility: After 90 days of employment
- Enrollment: Automatic at 3%, can opt out or change

**Employee Contributions**:
- Minimum: 1% of salary
- Maximum: IRS limit ($23,000 in 2024, $30,500 if age 50+)
- Contribution types: Pre-tax, Roth (after-tax), or combination
- Changes: Anytime, effective next pay period

**Company Match**:
- Match rate: 50% of first 6% you contribute
- Example: If you contribute 6%, company adds 3%
- Vesting schedule: 4-year graded vesting
  - Year 1: 0% vested
  - Year 2: 25% vested
  - Year 3: 50% vested
  - Year 4: 75% vested
  - Year 5: 100% vested

**Investment Options**:
- Target date funds (2025, 2030, 2035, 2040, 2045, 2050, 2055, 2060)
- Stock funds (large cap, small cap, international)
- Bond funds (government, corporate)
- Money market funds
- Contoso company stock (up to 20% of portfolio)

**Loans and Withdrawals**:
- Loans: Available after 1 year, up to 50% of vested balance
- Hardship withdrawals: Available with documentation
- In-service withdrawals: Not available before age 59Â½

## Section 5: Paid Time Off (PTO)

### Vacation Time
Vacation accrues monthly based on tenure:

**Accrual Schedule**:
| Years of Service | Annual Days | Monthly Accrual | Hours per Paycheck |
|-----------------|-------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| 0-2 years | 15 days | 1.25 days | 4.62 hours |
| 3-5 years | 20 days | 1.67 days | 6.15 hours |
| 6-10 years | 25 days | 2.08 days | 7.69 hours |
| 11+ years | 30 days | 2.5 days | 9.23 hours |

**PTO Rules**:
- Begin accruing immediately upon hire
- Available for use after 90 days
- Must request and receive manager approval
- Minimum increment: 4 hours (half day)
- Maximum carryover: 5 days (40 hours) to next year
- Payout: Unused PTO paid out upon termination (up to carryover limit)

### Sick Leave
- Accrual: 10 days (80 hours) per year
- Availability: Accrued monthly (0.83 days or 6.67 hours per month)
- Usage: Personal illness, medical appointments, family care
- Carryover: Unlimited (no cap on accrual)
- Payout: Not paid out upon termination

### Holidays
Contoso observes **11 paid holidays**:
1. New Year's Day (January 1)
2. Martin Luther King Jr. Day (3rd Monday in January)
3. Presidents' Day (3rd Monday in February)
4. Memorial Day (Last Monday in May)
5. Juneteenth (June 19)
6. Independence Day (July 4)
7. Labor Day (1st Monday in September)
8. Thanksgiving Day (4th Thursday in November)
9. Day after Thanksgiving
10. Christmas Eve (December 24)
11. Christmas Day (December 25)

**Holiday Rules**:
- If holiday falls on Saturday: Observed Friday
- If holiday falls on Sunday: Observed Monday
- Must be actively employed on holiday to receive pay

### Parental Leave
**Maternity Leave**:
- 16 weeks paid leave at 100% salary
- Must be taken consecutively
- Can be combined with short-term disability (additional 6-8 weeks)
- FMLA protection for 12 weeks

**Paternity Leave**:
- 8 weeks paid leave at 100% salary
- Can be taken intermittently within first year
- FMLA protection applies

**Adoption Leave**:
- 12 weeks paid leave at 100% salary
- Available for domestic or international adoption
- Must be taken within first year of placement

**Eligibility**: Must be employed for 12 months prior to leave

### Bereavement Leave
- Immediate family (spouse, child, parent, sibling): 5 days paid
- Extended family (grandparent, in-law, aunt, uncle): 3 days paid
- Additional unpaid leave available upon request

### Jury Duty
- Paid leave for duration of jury service
- Must provide copy of jury summons to HR
- Return to work if dismissed early from jury duty

## Section 6: Life and Disability Insurance

### Life Insurance
**Basic Life Insurance (Company-Paid)**:
- Coverage amount: 1x annual salary (rounded to nearest $1,000)
- Maximum benefit: $500,000
- Beneficiary designation: Required
- No medical exam required

**Supplemental Life Insurance (Optional)**:
- Employee coverage: Up to 5x salary (max $1,000,000)
- Spouse coverage: Up to $250,000
- Child coverage: $10,000 per child
- Cost: Based on age and coverage amount
- Medical exam may be required for amounts over $250,000

**Accidental Death & Dismemberment (AD&D)**:
- Included with basic life insurance
- Same benefit amount as basic life
- Covers accidental death and serious injuries

### Disability Insurance
**Short-Term Disability (STD) - Company-Paid**:
- Waiting period: 7 days (illness) or 0 days (accident)
- Benefit: 60% of salary, up to $2,000 per week
- Duration: Up to 26 weeks
- Eligibility: After 90 days of employment

**Long-Term Disability (LTD) - Company-Paid**:
- Waiting period: 180 days (after STD exhausted)
- Benefit: 60% of salary, up to $10,000 per month
- Duration: To age 65 or until recovery
- Eligibility: After 90 days of employment

## Section 7: Additional Benefits

### Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
- Provider: ComPsych
- Coverage: Employee and household members
- Services: Confidential counseling, legal assistance, financial planning, work-life resources
- Cost: Free to employees
- Sessions: Up to 6 free counseling sessions per issue per year

- Pre-tax payroll deductions for parking and transit
- Maximum monthly contribution: $315 (IRS limit)

- 25% discount at partner fitness centers
- Partners: 24 Hour Fitness, LA Fitness, Gold's Gym

- Eligible expenses: Tuition, fees, textbooks

- Annual training budget: $2,000 per employee
- Eligible: Conferences, certifications, courses, workshops
- Approval: Manager approval required
- LinkedIn Learning: Free unlimited access for all employees

### Wellness Programs
- Annual health screening: Free biometric screening and $50 gift card

## Section 8: Flexible Spending Accounts (FSAs)

### Health Care FSA
- Annual contribution limit: $3,200 (2024)
- Eligible expenses: Medical, dental, vision expenses not covered by insurance
- Use-it-or-lose-it: Must use by end of plan year (2.5 month grace period)
- Claims: Submit receipts through WageWorks portal

### Dependent Care FSA
- Annual contribution limit: $5,000 per household
- Eligible expenses: Daycare, preschool, after-school care, summer camp
- Age limit: Children under 13 or disabled dependents
- Provider: Must provide Tax ID or SSN

## Section 9: Eligibility and Enrollment

### Eligibility
**Full-Time Employees** (30+ hours/week):
- Eligible for all benefits
- Coverage effective first day of month following hire date

**Part-Time Employees** (20-29 hours/week):
- Eligible for prorated PTO and 401(k)
- Not eligible for medical, dental, vision insurance
- Eligible for EAP and wellness programs

**Temporary/Contract Workers**:
- Not eligible for benefits
- May enroll in 401(k) after 1 year of service (per IRS rules)

### Enrollment Process
**New Hire Enrollment**:
- Timeline: 30 days from start date
- Method: Online through HR portal or paper forms
- Support: HR available for questions

**Annual Open Enrollment**:
- Period: November 1-30
- Effective date: January 1 following enrollment
- Changes: Can change medical, dental, vision, FSA elections
- Life/disability: Can increase coverage (may require medical evidence)

**Qualifying Life Events (QLE)**:
- Marriage or divorce
- Birth or adoption of child
- Loss of other coverage
- Change in employment status
- Timeline: 30 days from event to make changes
