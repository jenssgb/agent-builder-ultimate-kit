Team,

We need to finalize pricing for the new product before launch (Dec 1). I've analyzed competitor pricing and our cost structure. Here's my preliminary thinking:

COMPETITOR LANDSCAPE:
- Competitor A: $45/user/month (basic), $75/user/month (professional), $120/user/month (enterprise)
- Competitor B: $39/user/month (standard), $69/user/month (premium)
- Competitor C: $55/user/month (flat pricing, all features included)

OUR COST STRUCTURE:
- Variable costs: $12/user/month (hosting, support, payment processing)
- Fixed costs: $180K/year (engineering, sales, marketing overhead)
- Break-even: 450 paid users

PRELIMINARY PRICING PROPOSAL:
- Starter: $49/user/month (positions us mid-market)
  - Basic features
  - Email support
  - 5GB storage/user
  
- Professional: $79/user/month
  - All Starter features +
  - Advanced analytics
  - Priority support
  - 25GB storage/user
  - API access
  
- Enterprise: $119/user/month (minimum 100 seats)
  - All Professional features +
  - Custom integrations
  - Dedicated account manager
  - Unlimited storage
  - SLA guarantees

RATIONALE:
- Starter tier is $4 higher than Competitor B but $6 lower than Competitor C
- Professional tier is competitive with Competitor A
- Enterprise tier matches Competitor A exactly
- Margin: 75% gross margin at full utilization

QUESTIONS FOR THE TEAM:

@Sarah (Marketing):
1. Does this pricing support the "premium product" positioning in our marketing?
2. Will customers perceive value at these price points?

@Robert (Sales):
1. Can your team sell at these prices based on early customer conversations?
2. Do we need more/fewer tiers?

@Jennifer (Product):
1. Are the feature differences between tiers sufficient to justify pricing gaps?
2. Any concerns about feature allocation?

Please provide feedback by Monday, Nov 11. We need to finalize by Nov 13 for marketing materials.

RECOMMENDATIONS:

1. MINIMUM USER REQUIREMENTS:
   - Starter: 1-25 users (no minimum)
   - Professional: 1-50 users (no minimum)
   - Enterprise: 100+ users (minimum 100)

   - Month-to-month: Full price
   - Annual contract: 15% discount
   - Multi-year (2-3 years): 20% discount

4. TRIAL POLICY:
   - 14-day trial with access to Professional tier
   - Requires credit card (auto-converts to paid)
   - Allows full feature evaluation
