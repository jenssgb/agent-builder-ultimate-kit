# Employee Benefits Overview

## Your Total Rewards Package

At [Company Name], we invest in our employees' well-being and success. Our
comprehensive benefits package is designed to support you and your family's
health, financial security, and work-life balance.

## Benefits Summary

### HEALTH & WELLNESS

**Medical Insurance**
- Multiple plan options (PPO, HMO, HDHP)
- Company covers 80% of employee premiums
- Family coverage available (50% company-paid)
- $0 deductible PPO option available
- Nationwide network (Blue Cross Blue Shield)

**Dental Insurance**
- Preventive care: 100% covered
- Basic procedures: 80% covered
- Major procedures: 50% covered
- Orthodontia: 50% covered (adults & children)
- Annual maximum: $2,000 per person

**Vision Insurance**
- Annual eye exam: $10 copay
- Frames: $150 allowance every 2 years
- Lenses: Covered in full
- Contacts: $150 allowance (in lieu of glasses)

**Mental Health Support**
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP): 8 free counseling sessions
- Therapy coverage through medical plan
- 24/7 crisis hotline: 1-800-555-HELP
- Free access to Headspace and Calm apps

**Wellness Programs**
- Gym membership reimbursement: Up to $50/month
- Annual wellness bonus: $500 (complete health screening)
- On-site fitness classes (varies by location)
- Standing desks available (request through facilities)

### FINANCIAL BENEFITS

**401(k) Retirement Plan**
- Company match: 100% up to 6% of salary
- Immediate vesting of company contributions
- Roth 401(k) option available
- Low-fee investment options (Fidelity)
- Financial advisor access included

**Stock Options (RSUs)**
- Granted at hire and annually
- 4-year vesting schedule (25% per year)
- Restricted Stock Units (RSUs)
- Equity refresh grants based on performance

**Bonus Program**
- Annual performance bonus: 10-20% of base salary
- Based on individual and company performance
- Paid in Q1 of following year

**Employee Stock Purchase Plan (ESPP)**
- Purchase company stock at 15% discount
- Contribute up to 15% of salary
- 2-year offering periods

### TIME OFF

**Paid Time Off (PTO)**
- Years 0-3: 20 days per year
- Years 4-7: 25 days per year
- Years 8+: 30 days per year
- Accrues monthly
- Rolls over up to 10 days per year

**Holidays**
- 11 company holidays per year
- Floating holiday: Use any day that's meaningful to you
- Winter office closure: Dec 24-31 (paid, doesn't count as PTO)

**Parental Leave**
- Birth parent: 16 weeks paid leave
- Non-birth parent: 8 weeks paid leave
- Adoption: 8 weeks paid leave
- Flexible return (work part-time for first month back)

**Sick Leave**
- Unlimited sick days (use as needed)
- Can use for family member care
- Doctor's note required for 3+ consecutive days

**Sabbatical**
- 4 weeks paid sabbatical after 5 years
- 6 weeks paid sabbatical after 10 years
- Use for rest, travel, learning, or passion projects

### PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT

**Learning & Development**
- Annual learning budget: $2,000 per employee
- Access to LinkedIn Learning, Coursera, Udemy
- Conference attendance (1-2 per year, budget permitting)
- Internal training programs

**Tuition Reimbursement**
- Up to $10,000 per year
- For degree programs related to your role
- Required to stay with company 2 years post-graduation

**Career Coaching**
- 1:1 career coaching sessions
- Resume and interview prep
- Internal mobility support

### WORK-LIFE BALANCE

**Flexible Work**
- Hybrid work: 2-3 days in office per week (varies by team)
- Fully remote roles available (select positions)
- Flexible hours (core hours: 10am-3pm)

**Home Office Stipend**
- $500 one-time setup stipend
- $100/month internet reimbursement (remote employees)
- Equipment provided: laptop, monitor, keyboard, mouse

**Commuter Benefits**
- Pre-tax commuter account (up to $300/month)
- Free parking at office locations (where available)
- Bike storage and showers at select offices

**Family Support**
- Childcare FSA: Up to $5,000 per year (pre-tax)
- Backup childcare: 10 days per year (subsidized)
- Elder care resources and support

### ADDITIONAL PERKS

**Life & Disability Insurance**
- Life insurance: 1x annual salary (company-paid)
- Optional supplemental life insurance available
- Short-term disability: 60% of salary (company-paid)
- Long-term disability: 60% of salary (company-paid)

**Legal & Financial Planning**
- Free legal consultations
- Estate planning services
- Financial planning sessions (annual)

**Employee Discounts**
- Corporate discounts on major brands
- Apple EPP: Up to 10% off
- Dell: Up to 15% off
- AT&T, Verizon: 20% off monthly plans

# Company Policies & Procedures

## Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to:
✓ Act with integrity and honesty
✓ Treat others with respect and dignity
✓ Protect company assets and information
✓ Avoid conflicts of interest
✓ Report violations of policy or law

Full Code of Conduct: [Download PDF](#)

## Workplace Policies

### ATTENDANCE & PUNCTUALITY

**Office Hours**: 9am-5pm local time (flexible with manager approval)
**Core Hours**: 10am-3pm (required to be available)
**Remote Work**: Hybrid schedule (2-3 days in office per week)

**Tardiness**: Notify manager if running late
**Absences**: Use Workday to request PTO or report sick leave
**No Call/No Show**: May result in disciplinary action

### DRESS CODE

**Office Dress Code**: Business casual
- Acceptable: Jeans, slacks, skirts, polo shirts, blouses
- Not acceptable: Ripped jeans, shorts, flip-flops, offensive graphics
- Client meetings: Business professional recommended

**Remote Work**: Dress professionally for video calls

### WORKPLACE CONDUCT

**Harassment & Discrimination**: Zero tolerance policy
- All forms of harassment prohibited
- Report to HR immediately: ethics@company.com or ethics hotline: 1-800-555-ETHICS
- Confidential and anonymous reporting available

**Substance Abuse**: Drug-free workplace
- Alcohol at company events (in moderation)
- Cannabis: Follow local laws, but prohibited during work hours

**Workplace Violence**: Zero tolerance
- Threats, intimidation, physical violence prohibited
- Report immediately to security or HR

### TECHNOLOGY USE

**Acceptable Use**:
✓ Company devices for business purposes
✓ Reasonable personal use (email, browsing during breaks)
✓ Protect confidential information

**Prohibited**:
✗ Illegal activity
✗ Downloading pirated software
✗ Accessing inappropriate content
✗ Sharing confidential information externally

**Security Requirements**:
- Use strong passwords (12+ characters)
- Enable MFA on all accounts
- Lock computer when away from desk
- Report security incidents: security@company.com

### DATA PRIVACY & SECURITY

**Confidential Information**: Don't disclose to unauthorized persons
- Customer data
- Financial information
- Unreleased product information
- Employee personal information

**Clean Desk Policy**: Secure sensitive documents at end of day
**Data Handling**: Follow data classification guidelines
**BYOD**: Personal devices must be enrolled in MDM
